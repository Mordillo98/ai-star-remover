Privacy policy

The short version

Your images never leave your device. Everything happens in your browser. There is no server to receive, store, or lose anything you put in. No upload, no account, no image history.

How the tool works

When you select an image, it's read directly into your browser's memory and processed on a <canvas> element using JavaScript. The cleaned result is handed back to you as a download. At no point is the image transmitted over the network. After 30 seconds, or when you click CLEAR, both the original and the result are discarded from memory.

What we collect

  • Images: none. Read into browser memory, processed locally, discarded. Never transmitted anywhere.
  • Accounts: none. There's nothing to log into.
  • Server logs: none from us. This is a static site hosted on Cloudflare Pages — see Cloudflare's policy for edge-level routing logs.
  • Cookies: none. Not a single one, from us or anyone else.

Third parties

None. No analytics, no tracking pixels, no ad networks, no fingerprinting, no third-party scripts. The only external resources are the two alpha-map images bundled with the page — and those are static files loaded once.

Your rights

We hold no personal data of yours — nothing to access, correct, export, or delete. There's nothing to request, because there's nothing stored.

Changes

We may update this policy; the date above reflects the latest revision.
