§ CONTACT

Say hello

Questions, feedback, a privacy request — we'd like to hear from you.

How to reach us

Simplest is email:

hello@aistarremover.example

(Replace with your real address before publishing. AdSense needs a working contact on-site.)

What to include

  • Tool issues: image format and size, expected vs. actual. Don't send the image unless asked; email isn't private.
  • Privacy/legal: we don't store images (see privacy); most requests concern AdSense-held data, which lives with Google.
  • Press/partnership: a line on what you have in mind.

How we answer

Small, independent project. We read everything, reply as time allows — usually a few days.
