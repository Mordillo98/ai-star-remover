A tool that respects you by design

Free, private, no backend. The Gemini sparkle is a fixed alpha blend with a public map — so a browser-only tool can do a better job than a heavy server one, while keeping your image on your device.

What it is

AI Star Remover removes the visible Google Gemini sparkle from AI-generated images, entirely in your browser. No upload, no server storage, no account. The math is a precise reversal of the watermark's alpha blend, so results are clean for the specific mark it targets.

How it works

The watermark blends pure white into the corner under a known alpha map. We apply the inverse formula, pixel by pixel, in the Canvas API. Two alpha maps are MIT-licensed from the open-source gemini-watermark-remover project. Full write-up: How the watermark works.

What it doesn't do

  • Does not remove Google's invisible SynthID watermark.
  • Not affiliated with Google. “Gemini” is a Google trademark; we're independent.
  • Not a license to use images you don't have rights to. See terms and the legal explainer.

Supporting the site

The tool is free. Once approved, the site carries a small number of Google AdSense ads to cover hosting — one top banner and one in-content slot on longer pages. Kept minimal on purpose.

Openness

The algorithm is simple and published openly in our articles. The same logic is available MIT-licensed in the project linked above. We're not selling anything.
